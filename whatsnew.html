Borrow & Lend

With the KA$HL£SS app, you could soon borrow and lend money easily with the people you know. Both the borrower and the lender will be notified about it in a separate Borrow & Lend section of the app so that it won't be necessary to remember whom you have lent money to or borrowed from. The borrower can return the money at the desired time with just one click.

Auto-Debit

You can now schedule your payments and money will be auto-debited at the chosen time without the hassle of entering your UPI pin. You can turn the auto-debit option off at any time.
